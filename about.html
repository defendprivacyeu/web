WHO WE ARE.

"The disregard for basic human rights in financial regulation, left unchecked and unopposed, is turning into systemic financial surveillance. Our fundamental rights are not negotiable, for they are the basis for freedom and liberal democracy. If we do not act now, we are giving up on crucial freedoms such as privacy and the presumption of innocence, that can hardly be reclaimed once lost."

Alexandre Stachtchenko, Co-Organizer Defend Privacy EU
